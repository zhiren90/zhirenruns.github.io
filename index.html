bio

Welcome! My name is Zhiren. I am a solid mechanician, currently working as a visiting scientist at University of Michigan.

My ongoing work utilizes continuum mechanics and viscoelasticity theories to design experiments for dynamic behaviors of soft biological systems. I am interested particularly interested in optic-based methods to measure deformation and stress.

My side passion is the history of mechanics and physics. A long-term project is to build upon Timoshenko's work in History of Strength of Materials and review the evolution of solid mechanics during the 20th and early 21st centuries.

I am a half-serious runner who tries to run a full marathon every fall. Feel free to get in touch if you would like to join me on my daily runs.
